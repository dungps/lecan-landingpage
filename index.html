We Are Changing Farming For Good

Milton’s Local is a proud provider of all-natural meat. We are named in honor of Milton Faison, a second generation farmer, and grandfather of our founder, M. James Faison.

Our mission is to support sustainable agriculture by creating wholesale channels for family farmers.
